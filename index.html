Our Services

Welcome to A&R Services, we specialize in delivering services tailored to meet the unique requirements of your business. From office cleaning to revitalizing your carpets, floor stripping & waxing, and commercial & industrial painting, we have you covered. Our team is committed to ensuring a clean and welcoming environment for your employees and customers.

Commercial Cleaning

Carpet Cleaning

We use advanced equipment and techniques to remove dirt, stains, and odors from carpets in high-traffic areas, offices, and other commercial spaces.

Office Cleaning

Our team will ensure that all areas of your office are thoroughly cleaned, including workspaces, break rooms, bathrooms, and common areas.

Floor Stripping & Waxing

Floors can become dull, scratched, and discolored. We specialize in stripping away old wax and debris, deep clean the floors, and apply a fresh layer of high-quality wax. This process not only restores the shine and luster of your floors but also provides a protective barrier against dirt, stains, and wear and tear.

Commercial & Industrial Painting

Our range of services includes interior and exterior painting, wallcoverings, and coatings. We also offer pressure washing to ensure that surfaces are properly prepared before painting.
